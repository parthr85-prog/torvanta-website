Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Overview

At Torvanta, we are committed to customer satisfaction. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a refund process as outlined in this policy. Please read the following terms carefully.

2. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • Request a refund within 30 days from the date of purchase
  • Provide proof of purchase (order ID, receipt, or transaction confirmation)
  • The product or service must not have been misused or damaged due to mishandling
  • Digital products must not have been fully downloaded or consumed
  • Services must not have been fully utilized or completed
  • The refund must be requested before the expiration date of the service or product

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Digital products that have been fully accessed, downloaded, or used
  • Services that have been completely delivered or utilized
  • Custom or personalized products
  • Products purchased during special promotions marked as non-refundable
  • Shipping fees (unless due to our error)
  • Items damaged due to customer negligence or misuse

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@torvanta.in with your order ID
  2. Provide a detailed reason for the refund request
  3. Submit proof of purchase and any relevant documentation
  4. Our team will review your request within 5-7 business days
  5. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method
  6. Refunds typically appear in your account within 7-14 business days

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if:

  • You have partially used a service or product
  • Additional services have been rendered beyond the initial purchase
  • Shipping costs are involved in the return

The amount of the partial refund will be determined based on the extent of use and our refund policy guidelines.

6. Refund Timeline

  • Request Submission: Within 30 days of purchase
  • Review Period: 5-7 business days
  • Processing Time: 7-14 business days after approval
  • Bank Processing: May vary depending on your financial institution

7. Return Shipping

For physical products, return shipping costs may be:

  • Covered by Torvanta if the return is due to our error or defect
  • The responsibility of the customer if the return is due to change of mind
  • Negotiable on a case-by-case basis

8. Special Circumstances

Refunds may be subject to additional review in cases of:

  • Purchases made with promotional codes or discounts
  • Bulk orders
  • International orders
  • Orders with special conditions or customizations

9. Exceptions and Disputes

Torvanta reserves the right to refuse refunds in cases of:

  • Fraudulent activity or suspicious requests
  • Violations of our Terms and Conditions
  • Abuse of our refund policy
  • Items returned in unsaleable condition

11. Policy Changes

Torvanta reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website. Purchases made after any changes to this policy are subject to the updated terms.
