Request Account Deletion

Torvanta allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Torvanta account using one of the following methods:

  • Log into the Torvanta mobile application and go to Profile → Delete Account
  • Send an email from your registered email address to:

support@torvanta.in

Include the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your registered mobile number
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your Torvanta account will be permanently deleted
  • Your profile information will be removed
  • Your listings, bids and associated project records may be anonymized for legal compliance

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 3–7 working days.
