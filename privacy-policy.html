Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Torvanta ("we", "our", "us") operates a digital marketplace platform that connects construction companies, contractors and labour contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use the Torvanta mobile application or website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, business name, and contact information
  • Email address and mobile number
  • Location details for project listings
  • Business registration details (if applicable)
  • Payment and subscription information
  • Device information and app usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Create and manage your Torvanta account
  • Facilitate bidding and project listings
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Send service notifications and updates

4. Data Sharing

Torvanta does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • Between users to facilitate project bidding
  • With payment processors for subscription services
  • When required by law or government authority

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data including encryption, secure authentication and restricted database access. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

6. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining confidentiality of their login credentials. Torvanta is not liable for unauthorized account access caused by user negligence.

7. Data Retention

User data is retained as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

8. Children's Privacy

Torvanta services are intended for users above 18 years of age.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

Torvanta may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
